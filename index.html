About Us

Better health for the people of Bangladesh with home delivery health check service from Japan
We are the first company in Bangladesh to provide a health check service by visiting your home.
Chronic disease such as diabetes and high blood pressure is a problem in many countries including Bangladesh. In In Japan, people take an annual health check that the government defines by law.
We aspire to be the pioneer of preventive health care and health support in Bangladesh by providing a health check system supervised by Japanese physicians and companies.

"Best in class quality health check-up from Japan"
Our service is supervised by Dr. Morita has a Medical degree from The University of Tokyo, a top school in Japan.
He supervises from service development to quality control. Staff in Bangladesh is trained by the Japanese staff to learn the Japanese system. We have established a system for you to receive our services safely.

Our Team

Our hope is to contribute to your health through our health check delivery service.
Through my experience as a doctor in Japan, I keenly aware of the importance of prevention against non-infectious diseases such as hypertension, hyperlipidemia, or diabetes.
In Bangladesh, according to research reports, the number of patients with non-infectious diseases is expected to increase rapidly in the near future.
To prevent these chronic non-infectious diseases, annual health check screening is the best way to prevent.
We can delivery health check services to your home or office which has as good quality as those in hospitals.
I recommend our service with confidence to keep your health efficiently and conveniently.
We would like to contribute to your healthy life and further development of Bangladesh.
