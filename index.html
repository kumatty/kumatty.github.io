About Us

"Better health for the people in Bangladesh with our home delivery health check service from Japan"


We are the first company in Bangladesh to provide a health check service by visiting your home.
Chronic diseases such as diabetes and high blood pressure are a problem in many countries including Bangladesh. In Japan, people take an annual health check that the government determines by law.
We aspire to be the pioneer of preventive health care and health support in Bangladesh by providing a health check system supervised by Japanese physicians and companies.

"Best in class quality health check-up from Japan"


Our service is supervised by Dr. Morita who has a medical degree in The University of Tokyo, a top school in Japan.
He supervises from service development to quality control. Staffs in Bangladesh are trained by the Japanese staffs to learn the Japanese system. We have established a system for you to receive our services safely.

Our Team

"My Sheba Anywhere" was developed by the technological and medical support of miup Inc., a healthcare IT start up from The University of Tokyo, and Bangladeshi medical staff "4billion health Ltd,."(subsidiary company of miup)

miup Inc. was established to provide healthcare for the people in Japan and Bangladesh and other Asian countries.
We have Japanese staff located in Bangladesh for service development, and continue to create new services for the people in Bangladesh.

Tomohiro Morita
"Our hope is to contribute to your health through our health check delivery service. Through my experience as a doctor in Japan, I'm keenly aware of the importance of prevention against non-infectious diseases such as hypertension, hyperlipidemia or diabetes. In Bangladesh, according to research reports, the number of patients with non-infectious diseases is expected to increase rapidly in the near future. To prevent these chronic non-infectious diseases, annual health check screening is the best way. We can delivery health check services to your home or office which have as good quality as those in hospitals. I recommend our service with confidence to keep your health efficiently and conveniently. We would like to contribute to your healthy life and further development of Bangladesh."
