OUR SERVICE & MISSION

We are the first company in Bangladesh to provide a health check-up service by visiting your home.

We know people are interested in to stay healthy and interested in health checkup, but it is also true that health check up usually takes a very long time and it’s difficult to find time... and that is why this service was born!!!
You can have a health check up while you stay at home. Professional medical staffs will visit your home and workplace instead of you having to find the time to visit the doctor.

Chronic disease such as diabetes and high blood pressure is a problem in many countries including Bangladesh.
In Japan, most people take a semi-annual/an annual health check up to stay healthy.
We aspire to be the pioneer of preventive health care and health support in Bangladesh by providing a health check system supervised by Japanese physicians and companies.
